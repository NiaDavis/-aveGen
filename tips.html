Tips

  • A penny saved is a penny earned
  • Saving money today is paying yourself tomorrow
  • Paid what you're worth and spend less than you earn.
  • Beware of little expenses. A small leak will sink a great ship
  • A budget is telling your money where to go instead of wondering where it went
  • Never spend your money before you have it.
  • Unless you control your spending, making more money won't help
  • Don't go broke trying to look rich. Act your wage.
  • It's not your salary that makes you rich. It's your spending habits
  • The quickest way to double your money is to fold it over and put it back in your pocket
